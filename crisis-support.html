Need to Talk to Someone? (Warmlines)

Warmlines offer free, confidential support from trained peers who have experienced their own mental health challenges. Whether you're in crisis or simply need someone to talk to, a warmline can help.

What to Know Before You Call

  • Call a warmline in your state if possible, though many accept calls from across the country.
  • Check the hours of operation, as they vary by warmline.
  • Some warmlines have time limits on calls, others do not.

Find a Warmline

Visit warmline.org to find a list of available warmlines across the United States, including their contact details and hours of operation.

Example Warmlines

State Warmline Phone Number Hours
AK Careline of Alaska (877) 266-4357 or (907) 452-4357 24/7
AL Wings Across Alabama (844) 999-4647 M-F: 2pm-8pm; Sat-Sun: 3pm-8pm

If you're in crisis and need immediate support, you can reach the Suicide & Crisis Lifeline by calling or texting 988.
